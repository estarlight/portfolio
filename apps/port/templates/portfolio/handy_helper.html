Handy Helper

Overview

Handy Helper is an online job board where users can create postings to hire helpers for small tasks, or to find jobs they want to be hired for. Job posters can create, edit, and delete job postings, and job seekers can view a posting, add a job to “My Jobs”, or mark a job “Done” once the task is complete.

Back-end validations ensure that there are valid inputs for login and registration.
